Mac and Cheese

Description

A hearty and filling dish that everyone loves.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil noodles, drain, and transfer to baking dish
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour over noodles, and stir
  3. Make the topping, spread it over the macaroni and cheese
  4. Bake until topping is golden brown
