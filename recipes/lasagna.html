Lasagna

Description

This delicious lasagna has lean ground beef, sweet italian sausage, and three cheeses.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatos
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzerella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 375 degrees F for 50 minutes
  6. Let the lasagna rest 15 minutes
