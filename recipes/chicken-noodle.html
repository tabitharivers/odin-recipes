Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

A timeless and comforting dish that's simple to make.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Celery
  • Chicken broth
  • Vegetable broth
  • Chicken
  • Noodles
  • Carrots
  • Basil
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Sautee onion and celery
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and bring to a boil
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes
